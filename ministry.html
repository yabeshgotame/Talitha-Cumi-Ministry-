Our Mission

At Talitha Cumi Ministry, we are called to proclaim the life-giving message of Jesus Christ. Our name, taken from Mark 5:41, reminds us of Christ's power to bring resurrection life to all who hear His voice.

"The Spirit of the Lord is upon me, because he has anointed me to proclaim good news to the poor. He has sent me to proclaim liberty to the captives and recovering of sight to the blind, to set at liberty those who are oppressed." - Luke 4:18
