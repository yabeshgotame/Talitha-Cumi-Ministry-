About Us

Our story, vision, and values

Our Story

Talitha Cumi Ministry was founded with a simple yet profound mission: to help people hear and respond to the life-giving words of Jesus Christ. The name of our ministry comes from Mark 5:41, where Jesus speaks resurrection life to a young girl with the words "Talitha cumi" - "Little girl, I say to you, arise."

What began as a personal YouTube channel sharing biblical insights and personal development knowledge has grown into a ministry focused on helping people grow spiritually and practically. While we don't claim to be experts, we're passionate about teaching others what we've learned from Scripture and life experiences.

Our Vision

To see individuals transformed by God's Word and equipped with practical knowledge for daily living.

Our Mission

To share biblical teachings and life skills through various platforms, helping people grow in all areas of life.

Our Values

  • Biblical Foundation: All our teachings are rooted in Scripture.
  • Practical Application: We focus on how teachings apply to daily life.
  • Continuous Learning: We're committed to growing alongside our audience.
  • Authenticity: We teach with humility and transparency.
  • Community: Building relationships through shared learning.
